Pricing

At Kenya Teaching Vacancies, we provide transparent pricing for all our services. We aim to make opportunities accessible to both job seekers and employers.

Current Job Posting Costs

  • All job postings are free for employers
  • Employers may optionally pay a token of appreciation if they hire a candidate

Future Paid Features

We may introduce paid services, including the Quick Connect feature for faster hiring. All transactions must go through the platform’s account for security.

Refund Policy

In case of unintentional transaction issues, a 75% refund is provided. The remaining 25% supports platform maintenance and improvement.

Accepted Payment Methods

Currently, we accept M-Pesa. Other banks or methods may be added in the future.

Disclaimer

Kenya Teaching Vacancies is not responsible for any transactions not completed via the platform’s account. All parties must submit proof of payment for security purposes.
